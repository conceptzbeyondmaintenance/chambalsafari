    ESG

    Built on a 500 year heritage, Mela Kothi-Chambal Safari Lodge is a venture designed to offer guests an immersive experience in the natural and cultural heritage of the Chambal Valley. We are committed to employing sustainable social and ecological practices, rigorously audited and validated in accordance with international standards.

    Our Environment, Social, and Governance (ESG) Report outlines the steps we are taking to minimise our environmental footprint, support local communities, and ensure responsible governance. It also highlights our ongoing efforts and future plans to enhance sustainability and contribute to the preservation of this unique region.
