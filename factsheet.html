    Fire Extinguishers

    There are fire extinguishers & brass bells hung outside... There are fire extinguishers & brass bells hung outside each set of cottages/rooms, and smoke alarms installed in all the rooms. In case of a fire, please exit your room and head for the main garden area. Please ring the large brass bell hanging in the main garden and/or in your accommodation block, to alert other guests/ team members. read more
