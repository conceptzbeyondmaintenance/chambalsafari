    OUR MEALS

    The Mela Kothi has a long tradition of hospitality and a well-earned reputation for its culinary delights. In its heyday, the raucous junior members of the family competed to present their unique twist to age-old family recipes, creating magical dishes that are still served with pride today.

    The lodge kitchen prepares authentic, home-cooked buffet spreads using fresh, organically grown ingredients from our own farms or procured directly from the local farmers. The menu features a rich and varied mix of local specialties along with contemporary Indian and Continental dishes....read more

    The beautiful old trees, expansive gardens, and the restored 18th-century stable block provide a picturesque backdrop for mealtimes.
