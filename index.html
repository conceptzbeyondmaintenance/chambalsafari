    The Chambal Safari is a journey through an unspoiled and unforgettable land.

    A land steeped in myths and legends, where timeless landscapes stretch across the horizon, rare wildlife breathes free, and magnificent ruins stand silent witnesses to the past.

    But above all, it is a land of gracious hospitality. A mere hour's drive from the Taj Mahal, in the heart of the Chambal Valley lies a sprawling oasis of tranquillity - Mela Kothi – Chambal Safari Lodge

    A warm and welcoming haven for the eager explorer and weary traveller alike.
